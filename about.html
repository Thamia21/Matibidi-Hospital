About Matibidi Hospital

Our History

Matibidi Hospital, located in Mpumalanga, has been serving the community for many years. As a public healthcare facility under the Department of Health, we are committed to providing quality healthcare services to the residents of Matibidi and surrounding areas.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide accessible, high-quality healthcare services to all members of our community. We strive to maintain the highest standards of medical care while treating each patient with dignity and respect.

Our Vision

To be the leading healthcare provider in the region, known for excellence in patient care, medical innovation, and community service.
